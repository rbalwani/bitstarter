by teaching we are learning

A massive open online course (MOOC) is an online course aimed at large-scale interactive participation and open access via the web. MOOC has been advanced with participation of many of the top university in the world. This provide opportunies for student all over the world to learn from the best professors from these universities.

However, in order to make MOOC successfully there is a gap that needs to be filled. Success rate of students completing these MOOC course is fairly low. Effectiveness of MOOC can be greatly enhanced by providing mentoring program where students can mentor other students by sharing there knowledge. MentorMOOC fill this gap by allowing students to earn badges and points by helping other students. These badges can be used to indicate depth of knwoledge in particular area.

Why should I participate in the MentorMOOC program?

  • Best way to learn is to teach others. This helps test your knowledge and answer questions from other will help your further explore topic from new angles.
  • You will earn badges and points which you can use in your online resumes.
  • You can earn certificate of accomplishment of MOOC with distinction.

How you can participate in MOOC program

  • You can mentor 1 to 5 students in the course and schedule google hangout sessions to review and answer questions.
  • You can prepare text handouts or video sessions which can posts on MentorMOOC to help other students.
